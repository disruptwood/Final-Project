## Slide 6: Scientific Validation (Theory)
**Why Verification Works: "Signaling Theory"**
1.  **Costly Signals**: Trust is built when someone invests effort (Cost) that is hard to fake.
    *   *Selfie* = Cheap Signal (Low Trust).
    *   *Marathon Medal* = Costly Signal (High Trust).
2.  **Homophily**: We bond with those who share our "Verified Traits" (e.g., Engineers, Runners).
3.  **The Result**: Verification acts as a "Social Collateral", reducing the risk of meeting a stranger.

---
### Critical Reflection (Slide 6)
*   **What & Why**: Connecting usage to Academic Theory.
*   **Critical Question**: "Is this theory overkill?"
*   **Honest Answer**: No, it explains *why* the product works. Without it, we are just another app. "Costly Signaling" is the scientific "Why" behind the "No Flakes" promise.

## Slide 9: Solution Strategy - The "3-Layer Trust" System
**Concept**: Reducing the "Cost of Connection" through Verification.
**The Pivot**: Shift from "Open Access" (High Noise) to "Verified Access" (High Signal).
**Mechanism**:
1.  **Trust Layer**: Identity Verification + "Social Collateral". *Solves: Safety.*
2.  **Relevance Layer**: "Level-based" Matching (e.g., Tennis 4.0, Career Stage). *Solves: Boredom/Awkwardness.*
3.  **Commitment Layer**: Reputation System ("Reliability Score") + Deposit for events. *Solves: Flakiness.*
